About Me

Currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Innovation Management, Communication & Data Science at Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne, I am a detail-oriented and inquisitive professional with a strong commitment to continuous learning and excellence in data analysis and digital marketing. Interested in data-driven decision-making and marketing performance, my experience as a Media Data Analyst at Richemont, combined with previous roles in web marketing and data analysis, has enabled me to develop expertise in data quality auditing, performance reporting, and digital campaign optimization.

My objective is to contribute to a dynamic and innovative environment where I can apply my analytical and technical skills (GA4, Looker, Looker Studio, SQL, PowerBI, Python) to help organizations maximize the value of their data and enhance their digital strategies.
